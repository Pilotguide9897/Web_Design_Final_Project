Rainbow 6 Seige

Ubisoft, Released December 15, 2015

Rainbow Six Siege is an online tactical shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft.The game puts emphasis on environmental destruction and cooperation between players. Siege is an entry in the Rainbow Six series and the successor to Tom Clancy's Rainbow 6: Patriots, a tactical shooter that had a larger focus on narrative. After Patriots was eventually cancelled due to its technical shortcomings, Ubisoft decided to reboot the franchise. The team evaluated the core of the Rainbow Six franchise and believed that letting players impersonate the top counter-terrorist operatives around the world suited the game most.
